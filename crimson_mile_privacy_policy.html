Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

Crimson Mile ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Crimson Mile mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use the App:

  • Account information: your name and email address when you register.
  • Workout data: workouts assigned to you, your logged results, effort ratings, and notes you submit.
  • Messages: communications between you and your coach within the App.
  • Performance data: personal records, event goals, and mileage you enter.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and operate the coaching service.
  • Allow your coach to view your workout logs, feedback, and performance data.
  • Send you notifications related to your training plan and coach feedback.
  • Respond to your messages and support requests.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a third-party database provider. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We do not sell your personal information to any third party.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — database and authentication (supabase.com/privacy)
  • Expo — app infrastructure and push notifications (expo.dev/privacy)

Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by emailing coachjay@crimsonmile.com.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date.
